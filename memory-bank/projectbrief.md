# Project Brief — SELIN "Into The Void"

## Project
SELIN — "Into The Void" Flowing Cards Website

## Description
Reverse Starfield effect: album covers and video clips flow in an infinite depth tunnel. Cards spawn near the camera (filling the screen), shrink via perspective projection, and vanish at the center vanishing point. Scroll down = accelerate, scroll up = reverse. Infinite loop.

## Target Audience
Music industry, fans, press

## Requirements
- Canvas-based Z-axis card rendering
- 50 album covers (cover-01.jpg to cover-50.jpg)
- Video clip support (short loops, DOM overlay)
- Scroll-based speed/direction control
- 3 color palettes (warm/cold/mono)
- Ring-based X/Y slot system (no overlap)
- Modular Z-wrap (no gaps)
- Fully responsive
- 60fps guaranteed

## Out of Scope
CMS, backend, e-commerce, multi-page structure
